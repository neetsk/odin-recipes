Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic recipe that can be enjoyed in any form you choose

This recipe will be for cheese pizza and toppings are optional

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Warm water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Basil (Optional)

Steps

Pizza Dough

  1. Dust surface to prevent sticking
  2. Combine Yeast, Flour and Salt in a large bowl
  3. Add warm water and olive oil and combine, add additional flour as needed
  4. Oil another bowl with olive oil
  5. Roll dough into a ball and allow to rise in olive oil covered bowl for 30 minutes
  6. Deflate then remove from the bowl and stretch to a 12" circle
  7. Drizzle olive oil over top and poke holes in the bottom

Pizza Assembly

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 and prepare a pizza lined with parchment paper
  2. Add pizza sauce on top of dough and spread evenly
  3. Mix together the Mozzarella and Parmesan Cheese and add overtop evenly
  4. Add additional toppings as desired and cook for approximately 15 minutes until cooked through or to desired crispiness
  5. Remove and let rest on a wire rack for 3-5 minutes then cut and serve
