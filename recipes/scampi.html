Shrimp Scampi

Description

Shrimp Scampi is a classic dish that I personally love to make.

I found this recipe through NYT cooking and have loved it ever since.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Butter
  • White Wine
  • Lemon Juice
  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to 2-3 minutes under package recommendation, set aside
  2. Brown butter in a skillet then add shrimp to cook
  3. Crush garlic and add to skillet
  4. Add white wine and lower heat to reduce
  5. When shrimp is cooked through, add pasta and seasonings, serve!
